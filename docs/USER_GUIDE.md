# User Guide

Welcome to AquaScope Kuching! This guide will help you navigate the features of the application.

## 1. Simulation Tab
The primary tool for flood risk analysis.
- **Sea Level Rise**: Use the slider to simulate water levels from 0m to 60m.
- **Tide Influence**: Adjust the tide slider to see how high tides in the Sarawak River compound flood risks.
- **Google Maps View**: Switch between different map styles (Streets, Satellite, etc.).
- **Layers**: Toggle "Land Parcels" to see property boundaries or "Hydraulic Infra" to see rivers and drains.

## 2. Market Tab
Browse real estate with flood awareness.
- **Property List**: View available houses and condos.
- **Auto-Locate**: Click a property to zoom to its location on the map.
- **Market Links**: Use the PropertyGuru or SarawakProjects buttons to see live listings.

## 3. News Tab
Stay updated with local land news.
- **Live Feed**: Click headlines to open news articles from DayakDaily or the Land & Survey Dept.
- **Search**: Use the "Search DayakDaily" link for specific housing news.

## 4. Map Interactions
- **Right-Click (Desktop) / Long-Press (Mobile)**: Drop a pin anywhere to see coordinates, elevation, and land parcel details (Lot Number, Title Type).
- **Search Bar**: Use the search icon on the map to find specific addresses or landmarks.
- **Hotspots**: Use the "Hotspots" section in the sidebar to quickly jump to key areas like Kuching Waterfront or Petra Jaya.
